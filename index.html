Strategic Business Models

Franchise Club aligns the brand contract with your exact financial goals.

Management Contract

The brand takes over full operations. Retain ownership and earn revenue share with zero operational involvement.

Revenue Sharing

Split revenues based on agreed percentages. Both parties share the risk and the upside equally.

MG Revenue Sharing

Receive a guaranteed minimum monthly income regardless of performance, plus an upside share.

Lease

The hotel brand leases your property for a fixed amount. Earn fixed rental income with zero involvement.
