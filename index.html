Choose the Business Model That Works for You

Franchise Club offers four models. We match the model to your financial goals.

🏨

Management Contract

The hotel brand takes over full operations. You retain ownership and earn revenue share with zero operational involvement.

📊

Revenue Sharing

You and the brand split revenues based on agreed percentages. Both parties share the risk and the upside equally.

🛡

MG Revenue Sharing

Receive a guaranteed minimum monthly income regardless of hotel performance, plus an additional share of upside revenue.

🔑

Lease

The hotel brand leases your property for a fixed annual amount. You earn fixed rental income with zero involvement in operations.
